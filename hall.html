The Hall of Wisdom


Welcome to the Abode of Learning

The halls call out to the wise - oh, come forth ye who seek knowledge of unlimited abundance, ye who dare cross into the realm of unparalleled intellect, ye who desire not only truth but divine insight into the mysteries of the universe... thus approaches the erudite before the Hall of Wisdom. In front of him stands the great giant of knowledge. Thus speaks the giant,

 "To enter these here Halls, you must best me in the game of logic. You may make one statement only. Climb thee onto my shoulders, then I shall speak nothing other than what you speak. If you speak the truth and I follow with a false statement, only then may you pass into the Abode of Learning. If however you are bested, you will be exiled to the Hills of Ignorance for thirty nights - a fair price to pay. However, if you dare attempt to confuse the Gods of logic by speaking in paradoxes, you will burn in the Depths of Illiteracy forever - an exact recompense."

Upon climbing atop the shoulders of the giant, he begins to ponder... how can the veracity of our statements be different if he will only repeat exactly what I say? Indeed, our hero is not wise. But you, dear reader, are among the wisest intellectuals of them all. So go forth and defeat the giant; enter into the halls of unparalleled erudition; step into the realm of unforeseen truth.
